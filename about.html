We Found The Solution For The Need Of The Hour


We knew this. Even before the pandemic, we knew that a large number of Indians struggled to pay for critical health costs,
that very few had insurance to fight health emergencies, and that one critical illness could deplete the entire family’s
financial health,sometimes even burying them in debt for years. More than 50 million Indians have already been pushed into poverty because of
healthcare related costs.we offer a more compassionate solution to those fighting critical illnesses and to empower
individuals from around the globe to help those in need.Today, we know better. We know that more healthcare financing options need
to be accessible to all Indians, that health emergencies arrive more often than we think

Our Mission


Make Healthcare Affordable To Save Lives Today, While Securing Families For A Better Tomorrow.


We are creating innovative technology and finance solutions to empower Indians to raise funds for medical emergencies and
critical illnesses and raise funds for a range of social and personal causes. Our platform has been used by lakhs to change lives
through timely contributions,fundraisers and actions.We make “smart giving simple” by curating a group of nonprofits that save or
improve the most lives per day. We aim to create a world where everyone has an opportunity to build a better life
and where there’s no suffering or death due to extreme poverty.
